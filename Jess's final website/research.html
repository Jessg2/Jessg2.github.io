Rynecki, J. (2018, August 15). 10 Years a DJ: A Story About Limiting Beliefs. Medium. https://medium.com/@j.rynecki2013/10-years-a-dj-a-story-about-limiting-beliefs-ff78789e57eb In this article, J. Rynecki reflects on their ten-year journey as a DJ and the limiting beliefs they had to overcome along the way. The author discusses how self-doubt and fear of failure can hinder personal growth and success, highlighting the importance of perseverance and self-belief in pursuing one's passions. The article offers valuable insights into the psychological aspects of pursuing a creative career and serves as a motivational piece for aspiring artists and entrepreneurs.
